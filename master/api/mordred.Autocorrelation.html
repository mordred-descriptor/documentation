class mordred.Autocorrelation.ATS(order=0, prop='m')[source]¶

Bases: mordred._autocorrelation.Autocorrelation

Autocorrelation of Topological Structure descriptor.

a.k.a. Moreau-Broto autocorrelation descriptor

\[{\rm ATS}_0 = \sum^{A}_{i=1} {\boldsymbol w}_i^2\]\[{\rm ATS}_k = \frac{1}{2} \left( {\boldsymbol w}^{\rm T} \cdot {}^k{\boldsymbol B} \cdot {\boldsymbol w} \right)\]\[\begin{split}{}^k{\boldsymbol B} = \begin{cases} 1 & (d_{ij} = k) \\ 0 & (d_{ij} \neq k) \end{cases}\end{split}\]

where \({\boldsymbol w}\) is atomic property vector, \(d_{ij}\) is graph distance(smallest number of bonds between atom i and j).

Parameters:
  • order (int) – order(\(k\))
  • property (str or function) – atomic property
Type:

float
