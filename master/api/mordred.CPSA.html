partial negative surface area descriptor.

\[{\rm PNSA}_1 = \sum_{a-} {\rm SA}_a^-\]

where \(\sum_{a-}\) means sum over negative charged atoms, \({\rm SA}_a^-\) is atomic partial surface area.
